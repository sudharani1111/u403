KLE Technological University, Dharwad

KLE Technological University
(KLE TU) was elevated in 2012 in Dharwad Karnataka in an ample area of 64 acres in lusty green and serene atmosphere on the lap of the hypnotizing Western Ghats from B. V. Bhoomaraddi College of Engineering & Technology which was started in 1947,
however, it was initially started as a school by the Seven Dedicated Teachers in 1916. Since then, it has been going through a lot of transformation from schools to polytechnic institutes to colleges to a university. Karnatak Lingayat Education (KLE) Society has over 250 colleges around Karnataka, Goa, and Maharashtra with a vision to be a society of education and research and empower the individual to become a successful person for the betterment of society and the nation.
It is a private university affiliated with Visvesvaraya Technological University and whose all UG programs are accredited by the NBA.

Faculty

KLE TU has 341 distinguished professors at its faculty, of whom 29.33% are Ph.D. holders and the faculty-to-student ratio is 1:15, that is, one professor is available for every 15 students.
